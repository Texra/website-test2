xTremeGamingx

I make various things on top of Matrix, such as the Telegram and WhatsApp bridges, the maubot bot system and gomuks, a TUI client for Matrix. I also occasionally contribute to other Matrix projects, like element-web and synapse. I'm currently working for Beeper (mostly developing my mautrix bridges).

I started programming because of Minecraft and I made quite a few Bukkit plugins before getting bored. I've also made a screenshot program, a web-based IRC client, a 2D portal-like game and other random stuff.
